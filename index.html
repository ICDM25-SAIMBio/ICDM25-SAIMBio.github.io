SAIMBio Workshop @ ICDM 2025

The Synergy of AI and Multimodal Biomedical Data Mining

Held in conjunction with IEEE ICDM 2025, Washington, D.C., November 12

Call for Papers Submissions Important Dates Organizers Program Committee Keynotes Agenda Contact

Call for Papers

The SAIMBio Workshop aims to bring together researchers and practitioners to discuss recent advancements, emerging challenges, and future directions in AI-driven multimodal biomedical data mining. We invite contributions on (but not limited to):

  • Deep learning for multimodal biomedical data (e.g., imaging, genetics, omics, metadata)
  • Large foundation models for healthcare applications
  • Multimodal fusion for diagnosis, prognosis, precision medicine
  • Explainability, robustness, and trustworthiness in medical AI
  • Biomarker discovery via large-scale data mining
  • Computational challenges in biomedical data science

Submissions

Authors are invited to submit original, unpublished work. Submissions should be formatted using the IEEE 2-column format (template).

  • Full papers: max 10 pages (including references & appendices)
  • Short papers: max 6 pages

All submissions will undergo triple-blind peer review. Accepted papers will be presented at the workshop and published in the IEEE ICDM Workshop Proceedings.
